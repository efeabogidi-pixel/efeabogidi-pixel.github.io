About

Efemena “Efe” Abogidi is a professional basketball player and developing GIS and spatial data analyst with an international journey shaped by both elite sport and academic growth.

Born in Nigeria, he came through the NBA’s global development pathway—training at NBA Academy Africa and later the NBA Global Academy in Canberra, Australia. Competing across different countries helped build adaptability, discipline, and comfort performing in high-pressure environments.

Alongside his athletic career, Abogidi has developed a strong foundation in GIS, mapping, and spatial data analysis. He is especially interested in work that connects environmental patterns, community conditions, and decision-making— with a focus on clarity, thoughtful analysis, and communication that makes complex spatial information accessible.
