About

Efemena “Efe” Abogidi is a professional basketball player and developing GIS and spatial data analyst with an international journey shaped by both elite sport and academic growth.

Born in Nigeria, he came through the NBA’s global development pathway — training in NBA Academy environments and later continuing his career through NCAA Division I and the NBA G League. Living and competing across different countries strengthened his adaptability, discipline, and comfort performing in high-pressure environments.

Alongside basketball, Abogidi has built a strong foundation in GIS, mapping, and spatial data analysis. He’s especially interested in projects that connect environmental patterns, community conditions, and decision-making — with a focus on clear communication that makes complex spatial information accessible.
