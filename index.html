Efemena “Efe” Abogidi

Efemena “Efe” Abogidi is a professional basketball player and emerging GIS & spatial data analyst. He combines high-performance sport with spatial thinking—turning complex geographic data into clear, decision-ready insights for real-world problems.

About

Born in Nigeria, Abogidi’s path has been shaped by international development programs, NCAA Division I competition, and professional basketball at the NBA G League level. That same discipline carries into his GIS work—where he’s focused on spatial analysis, mapping, and storytelling that helps people understand environments, communities, and risk.

He studies Anthropology with a GIS minor at Arizona State University (ASU Online), with a growing body of work in urban heat vulnerability, public health geography, and spatial equity.
